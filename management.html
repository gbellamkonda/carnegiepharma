President & CEO

Dr. Rakesh Grover

Dr. Rakesh Grover is the President & CEO of Carnegie Pharmaceuticals, LLC. Prior to the establishment of Carnegie Pharma, he was the President and COO of Sigmapharm Laboratories, LLC. Previously, Dr. Grover served as Director of Product Development at Perrigo Laboratories, Director of Research & Development at URL Pharma (formerly Mutual Pharmaceuticals) and Formulation Scientist at Teva Pharmaceuticals (formerly Barr Laboratories). He received his Ph.D. in Pharmaceutical Sciences from Long Island University, New York. Dr. Grover specializes in finding unique product development opportunities and then strategically and scientifically executing their development, submission and receiving approval in a record time. His experience includes leading the set-up of a vertically integrated company that did from manufacturing of active raw materials to selling the finished products under its own label. Dr. Grover has filed and/or brought several unique products to the market including a number of paragraph-IV projects and branded products.

Executive Vice President Analytical R&D

Keval Prasad

Keval Prasad is the Executive Vice President of Analytical Research and Development at Carnegie Pharmaceuticals. Mr. Prasad has more than 23 years of experience in analytical testing and worked closely with the metrology, compliance, information technology and regulatory affairs departments of the company. Mr. Prasad has participated in the filing of more than 50 ANDA and several NDA products. Prior to Carnegie, he was the Associate Director of Analytical R&D at Sun Pharmaceuticals, Inc. (formerly URL Pharma). Before that, he worked in the analytical departments of Teva Pharmaceuticals (formerly Barr Labs), Sandoz Inc. (formerly Eon Laboratories) and Apotex, Inc. Mr. Prasad holds a Bachelor of Science degree in Analytical Chemistry and Microbiology from Ryerson Polytechnic University in Toronto, Canada.
